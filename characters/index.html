Main Characters

Rashad

Rashad Butler is the main character of "All American Boys". Rashad is a 16-year-old boy who attends Springfield Central High with Quinn Collins and Noam "Guzzo" Galuzzo. After being profiled by a police officer he becomes a victim of police brutality leaving him hospitalized which sets in motion the plot of the book.

Quinn

Quinn Collins is the son of a soldier who died in Afghanistan and he faces pressure from many people around him to live up to the image of his father. During a night out with his friends, notably Guzzo, Quinn witnesses Rashad Butler being beaten by a cop in front a convenience store. This event leads to an internal struggle where Quinn struggles with whether or not he wants to testify in court.

Guzzo

Noam "Guzzo" Galuzzo is a close friend of Quinn Collins and brother of Paul Galuzzo. Guzzo is very close with his brother ad is fiercly loyal to him, this leads to him butting heads with other characters later in the book

Paul

Paul Galuzzo is a police officer who was tasked with working with Jerry's (a convenience store) after a recent string of robberies. After a misunderstanding at Jerry's where a store clerk believes that Rashad is stealing, Paul wildy over-steps his auhority and takes it upon hmself to show rashad "some discipline" by beating him to the point of hospitilization outside of the store.

Spoony

Spoony Butler is the brother of Rashad Butler. Spoony is seen as a dissapointment from the view of his father due to differing societal views. Spoony and Rashad are incredibly close and Spooy is one of the first and most enthusiastic people to fight for justice.
