Summary

*For more information on characters, please see the characters page*

The book starts wth Rashad Butler, a 16-year-old JROTC member, conversing with his friends on a Friday and discussing the plans for the evening. Later on, Rashad heads to the local convnience store for some chips before heading to a party with his friends. Unfortunately, due to a misunderstanding, Rashad becomes a victim of police brutality. This incident is also witnessed by Quinn Collins feels conflicted as he personally knew the officer. As Rashad recovers in the hospital, many of his friends and family are outraged that something like that would happen to an innocent person and they begin the fight for justice.
