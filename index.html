All American Boys

"All American Boys" is a 2015 novel by Jason Reynolds that follows a boy named Rashad who tries to seek justice against the cop that profiled and beat him and a boy named Quinn who struggles to decide whether or not to testify against his friend's brother.

Rating

7/10

"All American Boys" is a great book which touches on controversial topics such as police brutality and racism. The book does a great job at highlighting and emulating some of the racial biases and inaction that comes along with many similar real world cases. The book allows you to take a more personal angle with a case of profiling and police brutality. Many times in real life we see cases like this on a TV screen which adds a certain level of detachment, with "All American Boys" you get the perspective of the family, friends, and victim that adds a significant more amount of humanity which is very thought provoking. This book absolutey deserves the 7/10 ive given it.
